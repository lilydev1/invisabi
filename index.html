Invisible Brain Injury & the Long-term Effects of Meningitis

This organization was founded by an individual who was affected by meningitis resulting in a brain injury which was misdiagnosed and left untreated for over twenty years.

By bringing information on meningitis and acquired brain injury together on this website, survivors and their family can recognise and educate themselves on how to live with this Invisible Injury.
